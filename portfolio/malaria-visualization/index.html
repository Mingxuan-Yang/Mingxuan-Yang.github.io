Malaria Visualization

Sep 23, 2020
1 min read

Malaria is a fatal disease caused by infected mosquitoes. It has remained with human in a long history and is one of the biggest causes of death especially in the tropical regions. Almost half of the world’s population, according to the World Health Organization (WHO), is at the risk of catching Malaria. To gain more comprehension about this dreadful epidemic, 3 visualizations are created in this project based on the relevant datasets.
