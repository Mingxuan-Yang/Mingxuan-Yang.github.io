Dashboard for Doctorate Recipients in the United States

Nov 6, 2020 in PLOTLY • PYTHON
visualization dashboard plotly doctorate
1 min read

In this post, the main goal is to solve the following problems using plotly and dash visualization in Python. All of the doctorate degrees are pursued in the United States.

  • The number of doctorate-granting institutions and doctorate recipients over year. plot
  • The distribution of educational resources by state in 2017. plot
  • The debt level of doctorate recipients for each field of study over year. plot
  • The proportion of sex and field of study for doctorate recipients over year. plot
  • The median expected salary for doctorate recipients by employment sector. plot

The dataset is PhDs awarded in the US. Since the third and fourth problems have the same range of years, they can share the time slider and be visualized into the same dashboard. The Python codes can be found at the Github repository.
